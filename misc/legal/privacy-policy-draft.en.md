## Copy-Paste Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy for PulseWise

Effective Date: `[EFFECTIVE DATE]`

PulseWise is provided by `[LEGAL ENTITY NAME]` ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect personal data and sensitive personal data when you use the PulseWise mobile application, including patient and doctor features, related services, and support channels.

By using PulseWise, you acknowledge that your information will be handled as described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

Account and identity information

- Name, username, email address, password, one-time password (OTP) verification data, account role (for example, patient or doctor), and account status.
- If you sign in with Google, we may receive certain Google account information such as your name, email address, profile photo, and authentication token needed to complete sign-in.

Profile information

- For patient users, this may include date of birth, sex/gender, address, height, blood type, smoking-related information if provided, profile photo/avatar, and related profile preferences.
- For doctor users, this may include specialization, license number, hospital or clinic name, profile photo/avatar, and related account details.

Health and wellness information

- Health diary entries and wellness records, including symptoms, sleep records, activities, food consumption, nutrition information, body metrics, medication schedules, medication intake logs, and related notes.
- Health measurements and health-related entries such as heart rate, oxygen saturation, blood pressure, weight, BMI, and similar health indicators entered by the user or generated through app features.
- Machine-learning questionnaire answers, assessment inputs, prediction inputs, readiness checks, recommendations, and generated health reports.
- Emergency contact information that you choose to save, such as contact name/label, phone number, and priority status.

Health Connect data

- If you choose to connect Health Connect on Android, we may access health and fitness data that you permit, such as step data, exercise sessions, heart rate data, and sleep data.
- This access is optional and depends on the permissions you grant through Android and Health Connect.

Photos and files

- Profile avatar images you upload.
- Food images you capture or upload for food or nutrition analysis features.
- Files or report outputs you choose to export, print, open, or share.

Device, app, and technical information

- Device-related information such as device name/model, platform, app version, build number, locale, and time zone.
- Push notification identifiers such as Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) token, app installation ID, and related notification delivery metadata.
- Session-related identifiers and technical data reasonably necessary to authenticate users, keep them signed in, deliver app functionality, troubleshoot issues, and protect the service.

Data stored locally on your device

- PulseWise may store certain data locally on your device, such as session tokens, user ID, role, notification preferences, Health Connect sync timestamps, and other settings needed to keep the app functioning properly.

2. How We Use Information

We may use your information to:

- Create, verify, secure, and manage your account.
- Authenticate users and support login methods such as email/password, OTP verification, and Google Sign-In.
- Provide PulseWise features for patients, doctors, and authorized internal administrators.
- Record, display, analyze, and manage health diary, medication, nutrition, and wellness information.
- Generate health-related summaries, reports, recommendations, and machine-learning-based assessments or predictions within the app.
- Support patient-doctor connection features, including QR-based linking or patient access flows where applicable.
- Sync health information from Health Connect if you choose to enable that integration.
- Send reminders, health notifications, and service-related notifications.
- Upload and store profile avatars and process food images for nutrition estimation features.
- Improve service reliability, debug issues, respond to support requests, prevent misuse, and comply with legal or regulatory obligations.

We do not sell your personal data. We do not use your personal data for personalized advertising.

3. Permissions and Optional Features

PulseWise may request access to certain device permissions in order to provide specific features:

- Camera: used for optional QR code scanning and optional food photo capture.
- Contacts: used only if you choose to pick an emergency contact from your device contacts instead of entering it manually.
- Notifications: used to send reminder and service notifications.
- Health Connect permissions: used only if you choose to connect Health Connect and grant access to permitted health data types.
- File or media access: used when you choose to upload an avatar image or select an image/file from your device.

You can deny or revoke these permissions at any time through your device settings, but some features may no longer function properly.

4. How We Share Information

We may share information in the following circumstances:

With our service providers and infrastructure partners

- With our backend systems and service providers that help us host, secure, and operate PulseWise.
- With Google services used for sign-in and push notifications, including Google Sign-In and Firebase Cloud Messaging.
- With Cloudinary or similar media storage providers when you upload a profile avatar.

With authorized users inside the PulseWise service

- If you use patient-doctor connection features, information may be made available to the relevant authorized doctor account, patient account, or authorized internal admin personnel as needed to provide the service.

For legal and safety reasons

- If required by law, legal process, regulation, or a valid governmental request.
- To protect the rights, safety, security, or integrity of users, PulseWise, or the public.

User-directed sharing

- If you choose to export, print, open, or share a report or file, the selected destination, printing service, or third-party app may process that content according to its own terms and privacy practices.

5. Health Connect Disclosure

If you connect Health Connect, PulseWise may read approved data types from the Health Connect store on your Android device after you grant permission. Depending on the features you use, this may include steps, sleep, heart rate, and activity/exercise data.

PulseWise uses this information to support health tracking, diary synchronization, and related in-app health features. You can disconnect Health Connect or revoke access at any time through Android or Health Connect settings.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal data for as long as reasonably necessary to provide PulseWise, maintain your account, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and protect the security and integrity of the service.

If you request account or data deletion, we will process your request in accordance with applicable law and our operational, security, medical, and regulatory obligations. In some cases, we may retain limited information where required for lawful purposes such as fraud prevention, security, dispute resolution, or legal compliance.

Data stored locally on your device may remain until you log out, clear app storage, revoke permissions, or uninstall the app.
